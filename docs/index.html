Why PrismPlay?

Fast scans
Quickly index folders and get back to watching. Background metadata fetch with subtle caching.
Discrete by design
No cloud. Your library stays local. Optional achievements and stats are stored on your device.
Smooth navigation
Snappy browsing with instant search and sorting. Effortless library management without clutter.
Lightweight
Efficient UI with GPU fallback safety and a small installer. Works great on laptops and desktops.
